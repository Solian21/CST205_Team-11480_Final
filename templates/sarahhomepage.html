~ More about Sarah ~

I chose computer science as my major during my last semester of senior year. I had originally chosen another major but changed it last minute because I had come across a website that taught basic HTML and enjoyed it. Even before that I was always interested when people in movies would "hack" banks or important companies. It was a childish amusement that I had. When I applied to CSUMB I got an email about the CS++ Cohort. I was drawn to the fact that they guaranteed that they would give me the courses that was necessary for me to graduate with a Bachelors degree within 4 years. They also offered a certain amount in scholarships which would help with the tuition. When I started I saw that a lot of the assignments helped us learn both coding skills as well as career and critical thinking skills. Over my 3 semesters in the cohort I thoroughly found it helpful since it gives us a community to work with as well as TA's who are a year or two above us that are able to help us when we come across questions that we need answered about internships or about certain classes.

Besides coding I enjoy taking walks around my neighborhood or usually anywhere outside. Especially if I am with family. On my freetime I usually either read books or watch movies or tv shows. Currently I am watching Suits which is a pretty good TV show about a guy who was hired at a law firm without ever graduating from law school.
