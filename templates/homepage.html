THINK OF LATEER

We are all in the CS++ cohort 10 at CSUMB. Heading into college can be daunting especially for first year students, but the cohort program allowed us to acclimate to college with other people in the same major as us. CS++ is a cohort program designed to provide a community of other like minded individuals starting their freshman year in computer science. This program gives students access to guidance through the hard courses, access to study groups and other opportunities that are not available to others in the computer science major. Through the cohort, we were able to have early access to registration through a predetermined course load. This allows for the students to graduate in 4 years without fear of not getting the classes that they may need. Scholarships of up to $10,000 will also be offered to those that qualify in the cohort. Through the program we have access to career and internship preparation through their program Accelerate, a course that is made for preparing students for internships, open source and research opportunities. We are also able to build close relationships with the other cohort members going through the same program as us with the same course load. Networking opportunities as well as to allow you to learn more about the community of fellow computer scientists and understand the industry that you are going to be getting a job in in the future.
