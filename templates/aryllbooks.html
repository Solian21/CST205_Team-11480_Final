Books

I have been an avid reader since I was younger. I didn't really have much access to the TV during the day so I pretty much either played outside or read a book. More often then not though I was inside with a nice book. Due to the fact that I lived in Palm Springs at the time, it was often too hot to be outside during the day. My favorites at that time kinda shaped me as a person and what books I tend to gravitate towards now. These being The Land of Stories and The Series of Unfortunate Events. I loved these books so much as a kid and I still go back and read them from time to time when I need a pick me up. Books will always be something that I can find relaxation in. I think this is also important for me since it is something I can do that does not require me to look at a screen, which is helpful when studying in Computer Science because that's kinda what we are looking at all day. My current favorite books are Woven in Moonlight and The Cruel Prince series. I love a good fantasy what can I say.
